Thank you for your interest in getting in touch with us. To collaborate with our organization, you have two options at your disposal:

Collaborate with Us

Thank you for your interest in collaborating with us. We offer two main options for participation in our organization: Participation in open science projects and Project leadership. Additionally, we manage private projects under specific conditions. Below are the details for each option:

1. Participation in Open Science Projects

You can apply to join one of the available projects on our platform. Each project includes:

  • Mandatory skills: These must be demonstrated through a specific test for each initiative.
  • Recommended skills: Possessing these can increase your chances of selection.

Each project also has a maximum number of collaborators determined by its leader.

Requirements and Conditions

  1. Tools: An active GitHub account is essential, as all activities are conducted on this platform and related tools.
  2. Progress reports: Monthly reports are encouraged to document project progress. These reports facilitate tracking and award points to enhance participants' visibility within the community.
  3. Inactive projects: Projects that remain without reports for three consecutive months will be archived automatically.
    • To reactivate a project, a formal request must be sent to sciti.ceo@gmail.com with the subject: "Reactivate project: [project name]".
    • The request must include a work schedule of at least two months to ensure continuity.

2. Project Leadership

If you wish to lead a new project, please send an email to sciti.ceo@gmail.com with the subject: "New project: [project name], knowledge area".

Required Documentation

  • An image representing the project.
  • A detailed description of the project's objectives and scope.
  • A list of mandatory skills required for participants.
  • A list of recommended skills.

Additional Requirements

It is highly recommended that project leaders have prior experience in open science and proven leadership and organizational skills, as they will be responsible for the project's success.

Approval Process

Once your request is received, we will respond within a maximum of 15 business days with:

  • Instructions for creating the necessary assessments.
  • Justification for the maximum number of allowed collaborators.

3. Private Projects

We also manage private projects. For these cases:

  • The project leader must send an email to sciti.ceo@gmail.com with the subject: "Private project: [project name]".
  • The email must include:
    • A list of associated institutions or laboratories (if applicable).
    • An image representing the project.

Publication Conditions

The project will be published with:

  • The project's name.
  • The leader's name.
  • Optionally, the associated institutions.

Confidentiality: We respect the privacy of private projects but require ethical commitment from leaders to ensure that these initiatives do not contribute to actions that negatively impact society.
